## 2. Guidance & flight control: Unity vs top missile scripts

### Unity Missile (your system)

Key characteristics:

* **Multiple guidance modes**: GPS, antenna, sensor, lidar, manual, satellite 
* **Flight phases**: climb, coast, reentry, terminal, satellite orbit, station-keeping
* **Gravity-aware logic**: planetary ascent, altitude hold, ballistic compensation
* **Target motion prediction**: samples target velocity and leads shots in terminal phase
